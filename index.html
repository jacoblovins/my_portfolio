About me

Hi, I’m Jacob Lovins and I’m currently a student at Georgia Tech’s Full Stack Programming Bootcamp. From the time I was very little, I’ve been a creative problem solver. I’ve always been interested in creating and taking things apart to see how they work. I enjoy building things whether that be hot rods, motorcycles, musical pieces, or web pages and apps. Some of my projects include a 1972 Datsun 240Z with a full drivetrain swap, a lowered and engine swapped 1984 Chevy C10, a custom Harley, and many others. I have a degree in music and plan to use the creativity I developed through that to build amazing websites and apps.

I’m a fast learner and strong problem solver who loves to take on challenges. I’ve learned a ton so far in this bootcamp and we’re just getting started! By the end of this 6 months, I hope to be well versed in building interactive web pages and implementing databases and using platforms and frameworks like Node.js and React.js.
